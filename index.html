Customer Flow

When you are ready to use Gloebit services with one of your customers, you will send your customer to Gloebit to authorize your application's interactions with the customer's account. If your customer is not yet logged into Gloebit, we will present the Gloebit login screen. After logging in to Gloebit, we ask your customer to authorize your application:

Users are comfortable with this authorization style from social networks such as Facebook. They understand that after they grant authorization your application or server will be able to directly use Gloebit services on their behalf with no further interactions.

Monetizing with gloebits

gloebits

Gloebits are a digital currency designed for in-app purchases, especially micro-transactions. Users can spend gloebits in any application intregrating Gloebit's Digital Currency & Transaction Service. Gloebit manages all accounts and transactions for you. Learn more.

Customer Experience

Gloebit accounts are universal. The gloebit balance in an account is available to all Gloebit-integrated applications.

After granting Gloebit authorization for your application, your customers can spend their gloebits with one click from within your application without leaving it. It's smooth and fast, and it keeps the user engaged in your application. For a clearer picture, see the consumer experience flow details.

Merchant Experience

As a merchant using the Gloebit services, you price and sell your products in gloebits.

When you sell products to your customers via the Gloebit transaction service, you receive gloebit proceeds in your merchant account. You can spend these gloebits with other merchants or sell them and withdraw the proceeds. Gloebit keeps records of all transactions and provides an exportable transaction history for your account. See our merchant experience flow details for more information.

User-to-User Transactions

If you would like to allow user-to-user transactions within your application, gloebits are the best way to do so. The Gloebit services enable small, trusted transactions, and using gloebits provides you with a new revenue stream from user-to-user transaction fees. Please contact us to discuss making this offering available to you.

Pricing
